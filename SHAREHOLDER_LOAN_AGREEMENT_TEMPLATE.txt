WHEREAS the Lender is a shareholder/related party of the Borrower;

AND WHEREAS the Borrower requires funding to support ongoing business operations;

AND WHEREAS the Lender agrees to advance funds to the Borrower on the terms set out herein;

NOW THEREFORE in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:


1. LOAN AMOUNT

   The Lender agrees to advance funds to the Borrower from time to time as needed for 
   business operations. The total principal amount outstanding shall not exceed 
   $_____________ without written agreement of both parties.


2. PURPOSE

   The loan proceeds shall be used exclusively for legitimate business purposes including:
   
   • Operating expenses
   • Vehicle maintenance and repairs
   • Insurance payments
   • Rent and facility costs
   • Other necessary business expenditures


3. INTEREST RATE

   This is an INTEREST-FREE loan. No interest shall accrue or be payable on the 
   outstanding principal balance.


4. REPAYMENT TERMS

   The loan is repayable on demand or as cash flow permits. The Borrower shall repay 
   the principal amount:
   
   (a) When the business has sufficient cash flow; or
   (b) Upon written demand by the Lender with reasonable notice; or
   (c) Upon dissolution or sale of the business.


5. ADVANCES AND REPAYMENTS

   (a) The Lender may advance additional funds at any time by e-transfer, bank transfer, 
       or other mutually agreed method.
   
   (b) The Borrower may make repayments at any time without penalty.
   
   (c) All advances and repayments shall be recorded in the business accounting records.


6. RECORD KEEPING

   The Borrower shall maintain accurate records of:
   
   • All loan advances received from the Lender
   • All repayments made to the Lender
   • The outstanding principal balance
   
   These records shall be available for inspection by the Lender upon reasonable request.


7. SUBORDINATION

   This loan is subordinate to all third-party creditors and secured lenders of the 
   Borrower. In the event of liquidation or bankruptcy, this loan ranks behind all 
   commercial debts and obligations.


8. DEFAULT

   The loan shall be considered in default if:
   
   (a) The Borrower uses loan proceeds for purposes other than legitimate business expenses;
   (b) The Borrower fails to maintain proper accounting records; or
   (c) The Borrower dissolves or ceases operations without repaying the loan.


9. GOVERNING LAW

   This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of 
   the Province of [British Columbia/Alberta] and the federal laws of Canada applicable therein.


10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

    This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes 
    all prior agreements, understandings, and arrangements, whether written or oral.
