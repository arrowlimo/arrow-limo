⚠️ IMPORTANT REMINDERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. **TWO SEPARATE DATABASES**
   • Local and cloud DO NOT auto-sync
   • Must manually sync when needed
   • Choose primary database (recommend local)

2. **SYNC DIRECTION MATTERS**
   • Local → Cloud: Office work goes to cloud
   • Cloud → Local: Remote work comes to office
   • Wrong direction = lose recent work!

3. **COORDINATE WITH TEAM**
   • If multiple users, establish sync schedule
   • Don't sync while someone else working
   • Communicate who handled last sync

4. **BACKUPS ARE AUTOMATIC**
   • Sync creates backups before overwriting
   • Backups in L:\limo\backups\
   • Keep backups at least 30 days
